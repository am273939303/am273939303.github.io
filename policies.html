Gym Policies

Liability & Risk

By entering Cowboy Iron Gym, you assume all risks associated with gym activities, including potential injuries. The gym is not liable for any damages or incidents that occur within the facility.

Membership & Guest Policies

  • Members may not share their barcodes/QR Codes. Unauthorized access is considered trespassing.
  • Day passes are available at the front desk or online.
  • Memberships are subject to termination for violations of gym policies.

Security & Conduct

  • The gym is under 24/7 surveillance.
  • Harassment, violence, or disrespectful behavior will not be tolerated.
  • Photos and videos may be taken, but professional content creators must seek approval.

Equipment Use & Cleanliness

  • Re-rack weights and return equipment after use.
  • Sanitize equipment before and after each use.
  • Improper use of equipment or damaging gym property may result in membership termination.

Age Restrictions

  • No children under 14 are allowed unless approved by staff.
